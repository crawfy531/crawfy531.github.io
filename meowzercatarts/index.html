Who am I?

My name is Jennifer Crawford and I am an artist. The reason why this website is called MeowzerCat Arts is because my online username is sometimes Cats531 and other times Meowzer, so I decided to combine it. I do digital and traditional cartoons as well as paintings. Another thing that I create is digital music. This website is a place where I keep a record of all of my art (organized by year). It is also a place where I will say if I am open for commission or not as well as how to contact me if you want a commission. If I am open for commission I will also display my current rates on this home page(I will not show my rates when I am not open for commision).

Am I open for commissions? No

My family has been my greatest supporters in my progression in art. They have only encouraged me to progress in art and even suggested making it my job. But I was also told that some hobbies are better off just hobbies and I feel like my art is like that. There was a point in my life where I considered trying to make digital art a career but honestly I see it more as a hobby and I don’t exactly enjoy doing it as a job. Concerning commissions, I will only take one if I feel like I can have fun with it and am interested in the concept. I also can’t be too busy or stressed at the time I take on a commission because it will cause unnecessary stress.

Jennifer Crawford 2021
