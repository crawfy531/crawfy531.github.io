Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to have a place online where I store all my art (old and new). This website will also be used for people who are interested in commissioning me to do some art. What will be included is digital and traditional drawings and paintings as well as some audio files of some of the music I have made electronically.

Audience

I have put out my art on online sources, some being facebook and deviantart, and I plan to link to this website in order to have them not only see a veriety of my art work, but also be able to have the oportunity to view commisson avalability and rates as well as contact information. My family and friend would also use this website if they want a place to see all of the art I have made.
